What the service does

Kestrel monitors the procurement pages your team would otherwise check manually, then flags the items that matter: new tenders, award notices, framework openings and buyer movement.

01

Early bid signals

Catch new opportunities and route them to the team before deadlines get tight or notices disappear into inbox clutter. Keep the commercial calendar owned by your team, not by the portal.

02

Source-linked

Every alert points back to the notice, buyer and category so commercial can verify without chasing links.

03

Triage by segment

Filter by supplier type, geography or category so the team sees high-fit notices first.

How the service works

The service checks NHS procurement surfaces, compares new notices with the last saved state, extracts the commercial details and sends the result into the channel your bid team already uses.

1

Track

Watch Contracts Finder, Find a Tender, NHS SBS, CCS and selected Trust or ICB procurement pages.

2

Filter and rank

Remove duplicates and low-fit notices, then rank the remaining signals by category, buyer and likely commercial relevance so the highest-value opportunities surface first.

4

Alert

Send the opportunity into email, Telegram or an internal bid feed with a plain-English summary.
